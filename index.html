Privacy Policy This app does not collect, store, or share personal data with external servers. All data entered by the user, including trades, notes, and images, is stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to third parties. Images are accessed only when the user explicitly chooses to add them to a trade. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or user accounts.
